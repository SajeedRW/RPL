Game Rules

Researchwire Premier League — Official Rule Book 2026

01

Tournament Spirit and Conduct

This tournament promotes healthy competition, sportsmanship, teamwork, and enjoyment. All players must maintain proper decorum on and off the field. Unsporting behavior, repeated arguments, misconduct, or refusal to follow rules will not be tolerated.

02

Team Composition

Each team must include both male players and at least one female player. All teams must field the required combination during the match.

03

Match Format

Each league match will be 6 overs per innings. Finals might be 6–8 overs depending on situations and allotted turf timings — announced by the organizers. Standard turf cricket rules apply unless stated otherwise.

04

Female Player Rule 🏏

The 1st over is compulsory for a female bowler from the fielding side. During the 1st over, the batting side must also ensure their female player is on the pitch as striker. This rule is mandatory for both teams.

05

Bowling Rules

6 overs per league match: 2 bowlers bowl 2 overs each + 2 bowlers bowl 1 over each. No bowler may exceed assigned overs without notice from the organizers. Knockout/Finals rules will be determined separately.

06

Umpire Selection

The umpire must be chosen by the fielding team and must be from a third team not involved in the match. The umpire must have above-average cricketing knowledge and sufficient match experience.

07

Umpire Authority

The umpire's decision is final. No player or team member may argue aggressively, repeatedly challenge, or go against the umpire's decision. Any dissent may invite disciplinary action.

08

Disciplinary Action & Penalties

Misbehavior, dissent, abusive language, or rule violations may result in: warning → temporary suspension → expulsion for overs → any other decision by the organizers.

09

Batting and Scoring Rules

There will be no declared runs. Overthrows are allowed. Runs can be scored by other standard cricketing practices only.

10

Boundary & Turf-Specific Rules

  • Ball caught directly after hitting ceiling/roof/side nets → OUT
  • Ball landing on the wide line → Not a wide
  • Ball reaching boundary after touching turf roof or side nets → Six runs
11

General Behavior Expectations

All players must respect the umpire, respect opponents and teammates, avoid unnecessary disputes, play with sportsmanship, and maintain a healthy competitive atmosphere. This tournament is intended to be inclusive, enjoyable, and engaging for everyone involved.

12

Standard Rules

Any rule not specifically mentioned in this document will be governed by standard cricket match rules, as applicable to turf cricket and as interpreted by the organizers.

13

CricHeroes Registration 📱

It is compulsory for all players to install the CricHeroes app and register. This ensures live match updates, score tracking, tournament records, and better coordination during the tournament.

The organizers reserve the right to interpret, amend, or enforce rules in the best interest of fairness, discipline, and smooth conduct of the tournament.
